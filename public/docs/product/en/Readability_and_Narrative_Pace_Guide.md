# How to Improve "Readability and Narrative Pace" in Your SEO Strategy

> Version: 1.0 | Written: November 9, 2025

---

## Preface

"Readability and Narrative Pace" measures whether your content is easy to read and whether sentence and paragraph lengths are appropriate.

Low scores mean:
- Sentences are too long, hard to understand
- Paragraphs are too long, exhausting to read
- Poor pacing, readers give up easily

---

## WHY - Why Is Your Readability and Narrative Pace Low?

### Root Causes

**Problem 1: Sentences Are Too Long**
- Average sentence length exceeds 20 words
- One sentence contains multiple ideas
- Result: Readers struggle to understand

**Problem 2: Paragraphs Are Too Long**
- Paragraphs exceed 400 words
- No paragraph breaks
- Result: Readers feel exhausted

**Problem 3: Monotonous Pacing**
- All sentences are the same length
- No short sentences to break the rhythm
- Result: Readers feel bored

---

## HOW - How to Diagnose and Improve

### Diagnostic Method

**Step 1: Check Sentence Length**
- Calculate average sentence length
- Target: 15-20 words

**Step 2: Check Paragraph Length**
- Scan for paragraphs exceeding 400 words
- These paragraphs need to be split

**Step 3: Check Pacing**
- Are there short sentences to break the rhythm?
- Is there variation?

### Improvement Strategies

#### **Layer One: Shorten Sentences**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Find sentences exceeding 25 words
2. Split them into 2-3 shorter sentences
3. Ensure clarity

#### **Layer Two: Split Long Paragraphs**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Find paragraphs exceeding 400 words
2. Split at logical transitions
3. Add subheadings

#### **Layer Three: Optimize Pacing**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Add short sentences to break rhythm
2. Use lists and bullet points
3. Add visual elements
