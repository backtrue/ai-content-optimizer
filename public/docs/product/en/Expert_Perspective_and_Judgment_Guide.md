# How to Improve "Expert Perspective and Judgment" in Your SEO Strategy

> Version: 1.0 | Written: November 9, 2025

---

## Preface

"Expert Perspective and Judgment" measures whether your content demonstrates professional knowledge and unique insights.

Low scores mean:
- Lack of professional terminology
- Lack of unique insights
- Content is too generic

---

## WHY - Why Is Your Expert Perspective and Judgment Score Low?

### Root Causes

**Problem 1: Lack of Professional Terminology**
- Not using industry terms
- Content is oversimplified
- Result: Doesn't appear professional

**Problem 2: Lack of Unique Insights**
- Just repeating others' views
- No personal analysis
- Result: Content is ordinary

**Problem 3: Lack of Comparison and Judgment**
- Not comparing different approaches
- No recommendations
- Result: Content isn't practical

---

## HOW - How to Diagnose and Improve

### Diagnostic Method

**Step 1: Check Professional Terminology**
- Are industry terms used?
- Are these terms explained?

**Step 2: Check Unique Insights**
- Is there personal analysis?
- Are there unique perspectives?

**Step 3: Check Comparison and Judgment**
- Are different approaches compared?
- Are recommendations provided?

### Improvement Strategies

#### **Layer One: Add Professional Terminology**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Identify industry terminology
2. Use these terms in your content
3. Provide clear explanations

#### **Layer Two: Add Unique Insights**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Analyze the topic from different angles
2. Present personal perspectives
3. Explain your reasoning

#### **Layer Three: Add Comparison and Judgment**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Compare different approaches or methods
2. Analyze pros and cons
3. Provide your recommendations

---

## WHAT - Concrete Action Plan

### Immediate Improvements (1 hour)

- [ ] Identify and add industry terminology
- [ ] Present personal perspectives

### Medium-term Improvements (1 week)

- [ ] Deepen unique insights

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How do I present unique insights?

**A:** Through:
- Analyzing the topic from different angles
- Presenting contrarian perspectives
- Supporting with data or experience

### Q2: How much professional terminology should I use?

**A:** Use moderately and provide explanations. Don't overuse to the point of confusing readers.

Low expert perspective and judgment means "content isn't professional enough." The key to improvement is using professional terminology, presenting unique insights, and conducting comparative analysis.
