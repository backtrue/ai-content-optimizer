# How to Improve "Answer Extractability" in Your SEO Strategy

> Version: 1.0 | Written: November 9, 2025

---

## Preface

"Answer Extractability" measures whether your content can be easily extracted by AI search systems as featured snippets or AI answers.

Low scores mean:
- Paragraphs are too long, hard to extract
- Answers aren't clear
- Format isn't suitable for AI understanding

---

## WHY - Why Is Your Answer Extractability Score Low?

### Root Causes

**Problem 1: Paragraphs Are Too Long**
- Paragraphs exceed 400 words
- Contains multiple ideas
- Result: AI struggles to extract

**Problem 2: Answers Aren't Clear**
- Answers scattered throughout paragraphs
- No clear beginning and end
- Result: AI can't identify

**Problem 3: Format Isn't Suitable**
- No lists or tables
- No bold emphasis
- Result: AI struggles to understand

---

## HOW - How to Diagnose and Improve

### Diagnostic Method

**Step 1: Check Paragraph Length**
- Scan for paragraphs exceeding 400 words
- These need to be split

**Step 2: Check Answer Clarity**
- Are there clear answer sentences?
- Are they easy to extract?

**Step 3: Check Format**
- Are lists or tables used?
- Is bold emphasis used?

### Improvement Strategies

#### **Layer One: Split Long Paragraphs**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Find paragraphs exceeding 400 words
2. Split at logical transitions
3. Ensure each paragraph has one main idea

#### **Layer Two: Highlight Answers**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Add clear answer sentences at paragraph beginning or end
2. Use bold to emphasize answers
3. Ensure answers are independently readable

#### **Layer Three: Optimize Format**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Use lists or tables to display answers
2. Add bold and emphasis
3. Use short sentences

**A:** Target is 100-300 words. Anything exceeding 400 words should be split.

### Q2: How do I test extractability?

**A:** Through:

- AI search system citations
