# How to Improve "Key Summary and Highlights" in Your SEO Strategy

> Version: 1.0 | Written: November 9, 2025

---

## Preface

"Key Summary and Highlights" measures whether your content provides clear summaries and key takeaways.

Low scores mean:
- Missing summary
- Missing key takeaways
- Readers struggle to quickly understand

---

## WHY - Why Is Your Key Summary and Highlights Score Low?

### Root Causes

**Problem 1: Missing Summary**
- No summary at the beginning
- No conclusion at the end
- Result: Readers struggle to quickly understand

**Problem 2: Missing Key Takeaways**
- No "Key Points" or "Summary" section
- Key points aren't highlighted
- Result: Key points aren't clear

**Problem 3: Summary Isn't Clear**
- Summary is too long
- Summary is inaccurate
- Result: Readers are confused

---

## HOW - How to Diagnose and Improve

### Diagnostic Method

**Step 1: Check for Summary**
- Is there a summary at the beginning?
- Is there a conclusion at the end?

**Step 2: Check for Key Takeaways**
- Is there a "Key Points" or "Summary" section?
- Are key points highlighted?

**Step 3: Check Summary Quality**
- Is the summary accurate?
- Is the summary concise?

### Improvement Strategies

#### **Layer One: Add Opening Summary**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Add 1-2 sentence summary at article beginning
2. Explain the article's main content
3. Promise what readers will learn

#### **Layer Two: Add Closing Conclusion**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Add "Conclusion" section at article end
2. Restate main points
3. Provide action recommendations

#### **Layer Three: Add Key Takeaways**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Add "Key Points" section in article
2. Use list format
3. Highlight the 3-5 most important points

---

## WHAT - Concrete Action Plan

### Immediate Improvements (30 minutes)

- [ ] Add opening summary
- [ ] Add closing conclusion
- [ ] Add "Key Points" section

### Medium-term Improvements (1 week)

- [ ] Optimize summary and conclusion
- [ ] Add more key takeaways
- [ ] Use visual elements to highlight points

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How long should the summary be?

**A:** Opening summary: 1-2 sentences (under 50 words)
Closing conclusion: 3-5 sentences (under 150 words)

### Q2: How many key points should I have?

**A:** 3-5 key points work best. Too many confuses readers.
