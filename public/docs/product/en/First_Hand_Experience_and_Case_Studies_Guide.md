# How to Improve "First-Hand Experience and Case Studies" in Your SEO Strategy

> Version: 1.0 | Written: November 9, 2025

---

## Preface

"First-Hand Experience and Case Studies" measures whether your content includes personal experience and real case studies, demonstrating the "Experience" in EEAT.

Low scores mean:
- Lack of personal experience
- Lack of real case studies
- Content is too theoretical

---

## WHY - Why Is Your First-Hand Experience and Case Studies Score Low?

### Root Causes

**Problem 1: Lack of Personal Experience**
- No first-person narrative
- No personal stories
- Result: Content lacks authenticity

**Problem 2: Lack of Real Case Studies**
- No specific success stories
- No customer testimonials
- Result: Hard to convince readers

**Problem 3: Case Studies Lack Specificity**
- Case studies are too vague
- Missing specific data
- Result: Can't be verified

---

## HOW - How to Diagnose and Improve

### Diagnostic Method

**Step 1: Check for Personal Experience**
- Does content include first-person narrative?
- Are personal stories shared?

**Step 2: Check Number of Case Studies**
- Are there at least 2-3 specific case studies?
- Do case studies come from real customers?

**Step 3: Check Case Study Detail**
- Do case studies include background, action, result?
- Do they include specific data?

### Improvement Strategies

#### **Layer One: Add Personal Experience**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Review your personal experience
2. Find 2-3 relevant experience stories
3. Insert them in appropriate places

#### **Layer Two: Add Real Case Studies**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Collect customer case studies
2. Write detailed descriptions for each
3. Include background, action, result

#### **Layer Three: Optimize Case Study Presentation**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Use "case study" format
2. Add specific data and results
3. Add customer testimonials

---

## WHAT - Concrete Action Plan

### Immediate Improvements (1 hour)

- [ ] Review personal experience and select 2-3 relevant stories
- [ ] Add these stories to your content
- [ ] Add at least 1 specific case study

### Medium-term Improvements (1 week)

- [ ] Collect 3-5 customer case studies
- [ ] Write detailed descriptions for each
- [ ] Add customer testimonials

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How do I collect customer case studies?

**A:** Through:
- Customer interviews
- Email surveys
- Social media feedback
- Direct outreach

### Q2: How detailed should case studies be?

**A:** Include:
- Background (customer's problem)
- Action (your solution)
- Result (specific outcomes)
