# How to Improve "Related Questions and Keyword Coverage" in Your SEO Strategy

> Version: 1.0 | Written: November 9, 2025

---

## Preface

"Related Questions and Keyword Coverage" measures whether your content addresses related questions searchers might ask and covers enough related keywords.

Low scores mean:
- Your content only answers the main question, ignoring related ones
- Lack of coverage for related keywords
- Unable to meet searchers' multi-layered needs

---

## WHY - Why Is Your Related Questions and Keyword Coverage Low?

### Root Causes

**Problem 1: Only Answering the Main Question, Ignoring Related Ones**
- Searchers typically have multiple related questions
- Your content only answers one of them
- Result: Users seek other resources

**Problem 2: Lack of Related Keyword Coverage**
- Not using synonyms and related terms
- Not covering "People Also Ask" questions
- Result: Can't be discovered by related searches

**Problem 3: Content Is Too Narrow**
- Focused on only one angle
- Doesn't cover different user needs
- Result: Limited ranking range

---

## HOW - How to Diagnose and Improve

### Diagnostic Method

**Step 1: Check People Also Ask**
1. Search your target keyword
2. Note all questions in Google's "People Also Ask"
3. Check if your content answers these questions

**Step 2: Check Keyword Coverage**
1. List 5-10 related keywords
2. Scan your content to see if you use these keywords
3. Calculate coverage percentage

**Step 3: Check Content Breadth**
1. Does your content cover different angles?
2. Does it address different user needs?

### Improvement Strategies

#### **Layer One: Add Answers to People Also Ask Questions**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Find 5-10 "People Also Ask" questions
2. Add a "Common Questions" section to your content
3. Answer each question

#### **Layer Two: Expand Keyword Coverage**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. List 10-15 related keywords
2. Naturally use these keywords in your content
3. Use synonyms and related terms

#### **Layer Three: Cover Different Angles**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Review your topic from different angles
2. Add new sections or paragraphs
3. Ensure coverage of different user needs

### Real Case Study

**Original Content (40 points):**
```
Title: "How to Choose Lutein"
Content only covers: brand comparison, price, effectiveness

Missing:
- When is the best time to take it?
- What's the difference between lutein and zeaxanthin?
- Are there side effects?
- What are food sources?
```

**Improved Content (85 points):**
```
Title: "How to Choose Lutein: Complete Guide"
Content covers:
1. Selection criteria (brand, price, effectiveness)
2. Common questions
   - When is the best time to take it?
   - What's the difference between lutein and zeaxanthin?
   - Are there side effects?
   - What are food sources?
3. Recommendations for different needs
4. Advanced knowledge
```
