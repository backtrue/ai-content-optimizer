# How to Improve "Author and Brand Identification" in Your SEO Strategy

> Version: 1.0 | Written: November 9, 2025

---

## Preface

"Author and Brand Identification" measures whether your content clearly displays author and brand information, building trust and authority.

Low scores mean:
- Author identity is unclear
- Brand information isn't clear
- Readers can't build trust

---

## WHY - Why Is Your Author and Brand Identification Low?

### Root Causes

**Problem 1: Lack of Author Information**
- No author bio
- No author's professional background
- Result: Readers don't know who's writing

**Problem 2: Lack of Brand Information**
- No brand introduction
- No brand background story
- Result: Readers don't trust the brand

**Problem 3: Lack of Contact Information**
- No contact details provided
- No social media links
- Result: Readers can't learn more

---

## HOW - How to Diagnose and Improve

### Diagnostic Method

**Step 1: Check Author Information**
- Is there an author bio?
- Is the author's professional background explained?

**Step 2: Check Brand Information**
- Is there a brand introduction?
- Is the brand's background and mission explained?

**Step 3: Check Contact Information**
- Are contact details provided?
- Are social media links provided?

### Improvement Strategies

#### **Layer One: Add Author Information**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Add author bio at the beginning or end of the article
2. Explain the author's professional background and experience
3. Add author photo

#### **Layer Two: Add Brand Information**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Create an "About Us" page on your website
2. Explain the brand's background and mission
3. Showcase brand achievements and case studies

#### **Layer Three: Add Contact Information**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Provide clear contact details
2. Add social media links
3. Provide subscription options

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How long should the author bio be?

**A:** 50-150 words is ideal. Include: name, professional background, main achievements.

### Q2: How do I build brand trust?

**A:** Through:
- Transparent background information
- Customer testimonials
- Achievements and case studies
- Clear contact information
