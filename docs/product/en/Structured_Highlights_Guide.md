# How to Improve "Structured Highlights" in Your SEO Strategy

> Version: 1.0 | Written: November 9, 2025

---

## Preface

"Structured Highlights" measures whether your content uses lists, tables, and other structured elements to emphasize key points.

Low scores mean:
- Lack of lists and tables
- Key points aren't emphasized
- AI search struggles to extract key information

---

## WHY - Why Is Your Structured Highlights Score Low?

### Root Causes

**Problem 1: Lack of Lists**
- Content is all paragraph text
- No bullet points or numbered lists
- Result: Key points aren't clear

**Problem 2: Lack of Tables**
- No tables for comparisons
- No tables for data display
- Result: Information is hard to understand

**Problem 3: Lack of Visual Elements**
- No bold or italic emphasis
- No boxes or highlights
- Result: Key points don't stand out

---

## HOW - How to Diagnose and Improve

### Diagnostic Method

**Step 1: Count Lists**
- Scan content and count lists
- Target: At least 3-5 lists

**Step 2: Check for Tables**
- Are there comparison tables?
- Are there data tables?

**Step 3: Check Visual Emphasis**
- Are bold and italic used?
- Are boxes or highlights used?

### Improvement Strategies

#### **Layer One: Add Lists**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Find paragraphs that can be rewritten as lists
2. Rewrite them as bullet points or numbered lists
3. Ensure each item is concise and clear

#### **Layer Two: Add Tables**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Find content suitable for table display
2. Create comparison or data tables
3. Ensure tables are clear and readable

#### **Layer Three: Add Visual Emphasis**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Use bold to emphasize key points
2. Use boxes or highlights for important information
3. Use different font sizes

---

## WHAT - Concrete Action Plan

### Immediate Improvements (1 hour)

- [ ] Find 3-5 paragraphs that can be rewritten as lists
- [ ] Create at least 1 comparison table
- [ ] Add visual emphasis

### Medium-term Improvements (1 week)

- [ ] Add more lists and tables
- [ ] Optimize table design
- [ ] Add charts or images

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How many lists should I have?

**A:** No fixed answer, but typically 3-5 lists work best.

### Q2: How do I create effective tables?

**A:** Ensure:
- Clear headers
- Aligned rows and columns
- Easy to scan

---

Low structured highlights means your content "doesn't emphasize key points." The key to improvement is adding lists, tables, and visual emphasis.
