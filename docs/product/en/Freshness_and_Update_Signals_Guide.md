# How to Improve "Freshness and Update Signals" in Your SEO Strategy

> Version: 1.0 | Written: November 9, 2025

---

## Preface

"Freshness and Update Signals" measures whether your content includes current information and clear publication/update dates.

Low scores mean:
- Missing publication dates
- Content is outdated
- No update signals

---

## WHY - Why Is Your Freshness and Update Signals Score Low?

### Root Causes

**Problem 1: Missing Date Markers**
- No publication date
- No update date
- Result: Readers don't know when content was published

**Problem 2: Outdated Content**
- Cited data is too old
- Content hasn't been updated
- Result: Google thinks content isn't fresh

**Problem 3: Lack of Update Signals**
- No mention of recent updates
- No version numbers
- Result: Readers don't know if it's the latest version

---

## HOW - How to Diagnose and Improve

### Diagnostic Method

**Step 1: Check Date Markers**
- Is there a publication date?
- Is there an update date?

**Step 2: Check Content Freshness**
- What's the most recent data in your content?
- Is there outdated information?

**Step 3: Check Update Signals**
- Is there mention of recent updates?
- Are there version numbers?

### Improvement Strategies

#### **Layer One: Add Date Markers**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Add publication date at the beginning or end of article
2. When updating content, add update date
3. Use clear date format

#### **Layer Two: Update Outdated Content**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Scan content for outdated information
2. Replace with current data
3. Add update date

#### **Layer Three: Add Update Signals**

**Improvement Steps:**
1. Add "Last Updated" marker at the beginning
2. Explain recent updates
3. Add version numbers

---

## WHAT - Concrete Action Plan

### Immediate Improvements (30 minutes)

- [ ] Add publication and update dates
- [ ] Scan and update outdated information
- [ ] Add "Last Updated" marker

### Medium-term Improvements (1 week)

- [ ] Regularly check content freshness
- [ ] Update content quarterly
- [ ] Add update log

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: How often should I update content?

**A:** Depends on the topic, but generally:
- News: Weekly
- Tutorials: Quarterly
- Knowledge: Annually

### Q2: How should I mark updates?

**A:** Use clear format:
- "Last Updated: November 9, 2025"
- "Version 2.0 (Updated November 2025)"

Low freshness and update signals means "content might be outdated." The key to improvement is adding date markers and regularly updating content.
